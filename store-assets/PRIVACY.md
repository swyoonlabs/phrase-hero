# Phrase Hero — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-05_

Phrase Hero ("the extension") is a vocabulary-learning game. We respect your privacy.

## What we collect
**Nothing.** Phrase Hero does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

## Data stored on your device
The extension saves the following **locally in your browser** (via `localStorage`), so your
progress is remembered between sessions:

- Game progress (unlocked worlds, completion, scores)
- Your chosen meaning language (Korean / Japanese / Chinese / English)
- Your chosen theme

This data never leaves your device. It is not sent to any server, and there is no analytics,
tracking, advertising, or account sign-in. You can clear it at any time from the extension's
**Settings → Reset Progress**, or by removing the extension.

## Permissions
- `sidePanel` — used only to display the game's interface in Chrome's side panel.

No website content is accessed and no host permissions are requested.

## Contact
Questions? Open an issue at https://github.com/swyoonlabs/phrase-hero/issues
